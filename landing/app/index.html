Radio powered by SoundCloud

We bring the worlds’ musicians, podcasters, composers, instrumentalists and bands together into an open community that thrives on sharing. Even a child who is not a musical prodigy can unlatch new doors for the future music or contribute to the development of an entirely new genre of music. Uncovr is an initiative that helps budding artists and music enthusiasts cater and connect with each other by grasping the tastes of music through radio requests.

Better Suggestions

We help you figure out your music preferences by studying your listening habits and request types. The more you listen to music through us, the better we are able to recommend apt music tracks that you’ll enjoy. We trend personalized suggestions based on your favorite artists, the genre you prefer, etc. Our system presents you the best way to discover more about your music choice in real time.

Gain Beat Points

Your music fuel points multiply with the minutes you spend with Uncovr. Beat other users with the points you earn as it decides the accuracy level of our track suggestions. This is your chance to unravel which music is soulful to your ears and who sings to the real you.

Stay Connected

We are connected with Twitter, Facebook, SoundCloud, Hypem and the likes. Let us know about the kinds of musical performance you like through these sites and leave it on us to do the rest.

Thousands of Music Tracks

We are always on the lookout for new and dynamic music that we can add to our ever-expanding library of music tracks through the Internet stream. We trend artists, list top tracks and feature recently added ones to make our collection comprehensive.

Love it Everywhere

Let the music go mobile as you hit the road. Use your one-touch mobile application to tune into your personalized music radio station and whistle away.
